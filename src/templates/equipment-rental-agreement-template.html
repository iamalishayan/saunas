SCHEDULE B SAUNA RULES {{capacity}}-PERSON BARREL
There are serious risks associated with using a sauna. By using this sauna, you accept all health
and other risks and release the owner of the sauna from all claims, losses and liability arising from
your use of the sauna, including from burns and other injuries, health issues and death, however
caused.
1) CONSULT WITH YOUR DOCTOR. Use sauna only if and as directed by your doctor.
2) DO NOT use sauna if you have kidney problems, inflammation or acute infections, heart disease or heart failure, skin
infections or lesions, or problems with vertigo or dizziness. DO NOT use sauna if pregnant. No person with a
contagious skin infection may enter the sauna. This includes but is not limited to ringworm, impetigo, or staph/MRSA.
No exceptions.
3) DO NOT operate or use the sauna when intoxicated or on medication that affects your judgement, balance, reaction
time or capacity to endure heat.
4) Take care when moving within and around the sauna. Surfaces may become wet and slippery.
5) DO NOT attempt to move the trailer or move or adjust the position or structure of the sauna.
6) NO CHILDREN UNDER 12. Children under 18 must be accompanied by an adult.
7) MAXIMUM {{capacity}} SAUNA USERS AT A TIME.
8) DO NOT touch the stove, chimney or rocks. YOU WILL GET BURNED. Be careful when moving in and around the
sauna.
9) DO NOT apply water to stones when your skin is in range of steam. YOU WILL GET BURNED.
10) MAXIMUM 15 MINUTE SAUNA SESSIONS AND TWO SESSIONS PER DAY USE TIMER IN SAUNA.
11) Stay hydrated. If you feel unwell or uncomfortable, exit sauna immediately. No sauna heroes.
12) NEVER LEAVE STOVE DOOR OPEN. Stove door may catch, so ensure the stove door is fully closed.
13) Always monitor sauna and stove while in operation.
14) Only burn wood provided by the sauna owner. DO NOT use Firestarter logs, compressed chip wood logs or pellets. DO
NOT burn treated or painted wood.
15) To increase airflow to stove, you may leave catch pan (below stove door) open by maximum inch.
16) Empty catch pan after every use, INTO A FIREPROOF CONTAINER.
17) DO NOT remove catch pan while stove or coals are hot.
18) DO NOT start a fire without the catch pan in place.
19) USE ONLY ESSENTIAL OILS PROVIDED BY SAUNA OWNER. DO NOT APPLY OIL DIRECTLY TO STONES
FIRE HAZARD. Add up to three eyedroppers of oil to full water bucket.
20) SIT ON TOWELS IN THE SAUNA. This protects you and the sauna benches.
21) NO FOOD AND NO BEVERAGES IN SAUNA, OTHER THAN WATER.
22) NO RED WINE in the sauna at any time.
23) NO GLASS - Do not use breakable containers.
24) Do not sleep in the sauna or use it for any purpose other than for sauna bathing.
